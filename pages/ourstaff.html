OUR STAFF

DONNA QUINN

Owner/Director


Donna is a mother of two, wife, and Director/Owner of KidsZone. She has an Associates degree in Early Care and Education from Greenville Technical College and over 14 years of experience working with children from infants and preschoolers, to school aged children. Donna has worked in daycare centers, churches, nannied for a 3 year old drowns syndrome child, and operated an in-home daycare. Donna loves caring for infants, doing crafts with kids, and teaching through play. Kids are her passion and that passion is a big part of KidsZone.
