FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: ARE SNACKS PROVIDED?+

A: Yes. At no additional charge we do provide snacks throughout the day. Our snacks are peanut free.

Q: HOW DO MEALS WORK?+

A: When you check in you have the option to place a lunch or dinner order. Our meals are delivered by Outback. There is no specific time you need to place the order by.

Q: CAN WE BRING OUR OWN FOOD?+

A: Yes. We have refrigerators and microwaves.

Q: ARE YOU A PEANUT FREE FACILITY?+

A: No. Our snacks are peanut free but the food bought in or order from Outback may not be.

Q: HOW LONG CAN MY KIDS STAY?+

A: As long as you need. There is no restriction on how long your child stays at KidsZone.

Q: WHAT SECRUITY MEASURES ARE IN PLACE?+

A: We verify with a picture id at check in and check out. Parents are buzzed into our lobby and kids are buzzed into the center. No one is allowed into the center unless accompanied by a staff member.

Q: ARE IMMUNIZATION RECORDS NEEDED?+

A: Yes. You have 30 days from your first visit to get us these records. We do except immunization exemptions. Learn more about exemption forms here.

Q: WHAT IS THE CHILD TO STAFF RATIO?+

A: In our Nursery (12 weeks to 2 years) it is 1:5. On our floor (3 and up) it is 1:12.

Q: ARE YOUR EMPLOYEES BACKGROUND CHECKED?+

A: Yes. All employees are throughly backgrounded checked.

Q: ARE EMPLOYEES CPR AND FIRST AID TRAINED?+

A: Yes. All employees are required to be CPR and First Aid trained to work at KidsZone.

Q: WHAT IF MY CHILD IS CRYING OR GETS HURT?+

A: We will call you. If your child is crying for longer than 15 minutes or gets hurt we will call you to let you know.

Q: ARE CHILDREN SEPARATED BY AGE?+

A: Yes. Children 2 years and under are in the nursery room. We have an older kids section for 8 years and up. Our staff keeps an eye on all activities to make sure the kids are having fun and playing appropriately.

Q: DO YOU HAVE CAMERAS+

A: We will. They are schedule to be installed soon. When they are we will have enough cameras to cover the whole facility.
