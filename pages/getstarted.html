YOUR FIRST VISIT

STEP 1

Register - Online or In-Person

Registration happens only once. You can register online or in person during your first visit. If you register online your first hour will be free.

REGISTER NOW

STEP 2

Complete the DSS Form

Before you can drop off, we will need to complete the registration process. There are a few legal documents that need to be filled out and sign. For your convenience the forms you need are included below.

1. Fill out and sign the DSS 2900 Form.

    a. The DSS form takes some time to fill out. You can download the PDF and complete it on your computer. Print it out and bring it with your or email it to us at dropin@kidszoneupstate.com

2. You can sign our policies and waiver during your first visit or download them and bring them with you.

DSS 2900 FORM POLICIES AND GUIDELINES GENERAL RELEASE

STEP 3

Shot Records

You have 30 days from your first visit to bring us your shot records.

If you bring them for your first visit you can earn 2 free hours per shot record!
