GET STARTED

STEP 1

Register - Online or In-Person

Registration happens only once. You can register online or in person during your first visit. If you register online your first hour will be free.


REGISTER NOW

STEP 2

Come in for your first visit

Before your first visit, we will need to complete the registration process. There are a few legal documents that need to be filled out and sign. For your convenience, the forms you need are included below.

1. Fill out and sign the DSS 2900 Form.
    a. You can download a PDF of the form below and fill it out on your computer. Please don’t forget to print it and bring it with you.
    b. You can use one 2900 Form for multiple children. To do this you need to add all your children’s first names in the first name field.

2. Download, read and sign our Policies and Guidelines.

3. Download, read and sign our General Release and Waiver of Claims.

4. Bring Immunization Record for each child.

5. We will take your picture to store in our system for verification during check in and check out.

DSS 2900 FORM POLICIES AND GUIDELINES GENERAL RELEASE

STEP 3

Drop off your kids

You will need a Photo ID for Check In. Snacks are complementary but if you would like a full meal for your child, you can bring it. We have a refrigerator and a microwave and you may also order a meal from the Outback kid's menu.

STEP 4

Pick up your kids

You will need a Photo ID for Check Out. Payment will be collected during Check Out.
