CAREERS

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to help parents get more quality time with their kids by providing drop-in childcare that is Trusted, Convenient, and Affordable. We help kids have fun, be active, and learn through play in a safe, secure, and social environment. To do this we hire Greenvilleâ€™s most passionate and caring people. Nothing is more important to us than the care of the kids we have been entrusted with and that starts with a great staff.

Working at KidsZone is a fun and fulfilling passion. We are a team, working together to ensure that kids have a wonderful time and parents have a worry-free experience. At KidsZone kids are active and participate is many forms of play. At times this can be chaotic and loud. It is very important that our staff are infinitely patient, can remain calm in the face of the mayhem and loudness, and are passionate about children.

AVAILABLE POSITIONS

To work at KidsZone you must meet all DSS certifications, and be first aid and CPR trained. Assistant Caregivers will be able to work on their certifications while working at KidsZone as an assistant to a caregiver.

Assistant Director

To apply for this role, you must have at least 5 years experience working with children.

Lead Caregiver

To apply for this role, you must have at least 3 years experience working with children.

Caregiver

To apply for this role, you must have at least 6 months experience working with children.

Assistant Caregiver

While completing your training, you will be assisting the other caregivers.

HOW TO APPLY

1. Submit a resume (during assessment test)

    a. Basic contact information

    b. Work history

    c. Education

    d. Current certifications

2. Take the assessment test (start by clicking below)

Uniforms

Flexibility
